About 关于我们

EN: The Institute for Applied Process Thought (IAPT) is a non-profit platform inspired by Alfred North Whitehead’s organic philosophy. We collaborate with universities and communities to translate process thought into practical pathways for ecological civilization.

中： 应用过程思想研究院以怀特海有机哲学为理论基础，致力于把过程思想落地为教育、生态与社会创新的实践。

Values 核心价值

  • Relationality 关系性 · Creativity 创造力 · Harmony 和谐
