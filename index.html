Welcome

If you are looking for a hopeful view of the future, you have come to the right place. We are seeking to use an abstract set of principles to develop new methods of solving social problems.

Mission

The starting point is process thought, which envisions a web of relations in flux rather than inert matter. This philosophy shows that we can have the most enduring impact by focusing less on isolated events and more on the contexts in which they occur.

Focus

We agree with Marx that philosophy should change the world, not merely interpret it. Process thought has been around for a century or more, yet too often confined to classrooms. IAPT seeks to bring its practical significance into the public sphere.

Upcoming

We want to change that by showing the practical significance of process thought. Learn more in our Vision or explore upcoming Events.
